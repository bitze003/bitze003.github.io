Welcome!

Hello, I'm Kenneth Bitzer. I grew up in Northern Wisconsin on the shores of Lake Superior. I lived in there for 18 years of my life. I moved to Duluth Minnesota and started working at the 'Whole Foods Co-Op'. In 2011 I started school at the University of Duluth Minnesota. I began my studies studying geological sciences.

I graduated in 2015 with a BS in geology, I continued working in Duluth as a manager on duty for the 'Whole Foods Co-Op'. I then decided to move to Minneapolis Minnesota in the fall of 2016 and started looking for a job more inline with my major. August of 2016 I took a job as a field chemist.

I have worked as a field chemist for the past 2 years. I am currently seeking an education and a job in web development. I am currently enrolled in the Trillogy bootcamp, run through the Univiersity of Minneosota Twin Cities.
