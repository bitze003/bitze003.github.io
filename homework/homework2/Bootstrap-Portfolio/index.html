Welcome!

Hello, I'm Kenneth Bitzer. I grew up in Northern Wisconsin on the shores of Lake Superior. I lived there for 18 years. I moved to Duluth Minnesota and started working at the 'Whole Foods Co-Op'. In 2011 I started school at the University of Minnesota Duluth. I began my studies studying geological sciences.

I have lead and managed over 25 people while attending college and getting a Bachelor of Science degree in Geology. I am currently enrolled in the Trilogy web developer bootcamp taught by the University of Minnesota Twin Cities. I was recently promoted to project manager position for Carver County Environmental Center overseeing all chemical operations.

Schooling has honed my problem solving skills; being a manager has aided in my communication and customer service skills. I excel in high stress and complex situations. Good communication and problem solving have been key to my success. I have a large interest in new technology and science.The versatility of web development and the large complex problems that can be solved through back-end programming has made me want to pursue this new career.
